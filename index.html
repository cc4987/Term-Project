Why did I choose this idea?

I chose this idea because as a very indecisive person, I also know the struggles of picking a movie or finding something to watch that I know I'll love! I made this recommendations website to help people out with this issue! The database used has access to approx 45,000 movies! Check it out here: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/rounakbanik/the-movies-dataset

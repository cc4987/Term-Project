Project Notes

This project was made as a way to help indecisive people find new movies to watch, or anyone just looking for a movie recommendation!
I learned about the usage of cosine similarity while making the recommendation generator, and it was a bit of a tough process. It was an interesting approach to learning about the topic overall, and helped in my understanding of the process. The process of how this generator works is by loading and parsing through the database, computing cosine similarity with pairwise similarity, and finally printing recommendations that have high similarity scores!
